ABOUT US

Noxus Warlords is a League of Legends tournament organizer and community space hosting an organized competitive experience to players that are Emerald 4 and above. With our first tournament coming up in early winter we hope to provide a fun and competitive space for teams to compete. Our first invitational will be primarily pre-made teams, but individuals and groups of 2-4 are eligible to sign up as well.
