NOXUS WARLORDS: RULES AND GUIDELINES

"The moment justice bends, it breaks." - Kayle

General Rules

  • This tournament is for fun: be a decent human being. Respect other users' and their opinions. No racism, sexism, trans/homophobia, etc.
  • No spamming or disruptive chat. This includes EXCESSIVE CAPS and ping spams. Only Team Captains and Moderators can ping everyone.
  • No NSFW or obscene content unless it is within #nsfw-chat
  • No advertisements for other communities or streams. You can post content in the #media-channel if it's relevant.
  • Respect the general Discord Community guidelines within the tournament and in the Discord server.

Tournament Rules

  • The tournament requires you to be Emerald 4 or higher.
  • There will be preset teams: join with friends or find a team to join within our Discord server!
